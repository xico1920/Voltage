Unleashing Raw Power on the Track

Quantumm is Voltage's GT3 prototype, showcasing the brand's venture into high-performance racing. Unlike the classic designs that Voltage is known for, Quantumm is a futuristic marvel with a focus on speed and precision. Powered by a formidable 1000 horsepower and 1423 Nm of torque, this prototype accelerates from 0-100 km/h in a breathtaking 2.1 seconds, making it a serious contender on the track.

Futuristic Design Meets Aerodynamic Precision

The design of Quantumm is a clear departure from Voltage's traditional aesthetic, embracing a sleek and aerodynamic form that is purpose-built for racing. Every element, from its aggressive low profile to its advanced aerodynamic features like spoilers and diffusers, is optimized for performance. This car isn’t just designed to look fast; it’s engineered to maximize downforce and minimize drag, giving it a competitive edge in the GT3 category.

Cutting-Edge Technology with a Sustainable Edge

nside, Quantumm offers a driver-focused experience with state-of-the-art digital interfaces and AI-driven controls, tailored for high-performance racing. Despite its advanced technology, the car stays true to Voltage’s commitment to sustainability, utilizing eco-friendly materials and an efficient electric powertrain. This GT3 prototype embodies Voltage's vision for the future of racing, merging power, innovation, and environmental responsibility.
