Introducing the Voltage Dynamo

The Voltage E1 combines classic design with modern innovation, capturing the elegance of vintage automobiles while offering a sophisticated electric powertrain for impressive performance and efficiency. Externally, the Voltage E1 exudes classic charm with its curved lines, chrome accents, and iconic grille, evoking nostalgia and turning heads. Every detail is crafted with precision, reflecting the enduring appeal of classic automotive design. Beneath its stunning exterior, the Voltage E1 houses an electric powertrain delivering 385 horsepower and 450 Nm of torque, ensuring a smooth and spirited drive with remarkable efficiency.
