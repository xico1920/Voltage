Our Story

Founded in 2016, Voltage was born out of a passion for the past and a vision for the future. Our journey began with a simple yet ambitious idea: to create electric vehicles that marry the timeless elegance of classic car design with the cutting-edge technology of the modern era. We believe that driving a car should be an experience that evokes emotion, nostalgia, and excitement, all while embracing the advancements of today.

So much history yet so little time.

From the very beginning, our mission has been clear—to craft vehicles that are as visually stunning as they are technologically advanced. We drew inspiration from the iconic designs of mid-20th century roadsters, blending them with the latest in electric powertrain technology to create cars that are not just beautiful to look at, but also exhilarating to drive. Each model we produce is a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability.

As we continue to grow, our dedication to pushing the boundaries of automotive design and performance remains unwavering. Voltage is not just a car brand; it’s a celebration of heritage and progress, where the classic meets the contemporary in perfect harmony. Whether you're drawn to our modern interpretations of vintage styles or our bold forays into futuristic design, we invite you to join us on this exciting journey as we drive towards a cleaner, more beautiful future.
